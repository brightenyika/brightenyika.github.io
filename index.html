VeeMart Store Analysis with
power bi

VeeMart is a retail store chain with 11 stores across the U.S. They struggled to generate insights from unstructured Excel-based sales data. To solve this, I transformed the raw data into a structured Power BI data model that eliminated redundancy and enabled efficient reporting.

Through interactive dashboards and analysis, I delivered insights into sales performance, customer behavior, product demand, and store operations, enabling the management to make informed decisions and monitor key business KPIs.
